America’s Got Talent judge Sofia Vergara recently teased her new coffee brand by posting a nude photo of herself on social media. The new brand is called “¡DÍOS MIO!“ and is launching with three different roasts.

Sofia Vergara Teases Coffee Brand with Nude Photo

Vergara recently took to Instagram to share a photo of herself lying nude in a pile of coffee beans, which strategically cover her breasts. The actress added #tb to the post’s caption, suggesting that this is a throwback photo repurposed to promote the new brand.

She added, “soon enough you’ll be saying Dios Mío too.” This is a reference to her new coffee brand, which she later announced with a video advertisement in which she jokes about being a “victim of disgusting coffee.”

“I’ve partnered with passionate and dedicated Colombian women coffee growers — women who cultivate the coffee that I’ve always dreamed of,” Vergara can be heard saying in the clip, which directs fans to Walmart.com to buy the product.

Vergara also has a line of fashion (including jeans and swimsuits) and home decor (such as kitchenware and bedding) available to buy at Walmart.

The Coffee Is Available in Three Roasts

According to Variety, Vergara’s coffee brand is available in a light, medium, or dark roast. The ground coffee is available to buy in 12 oz. bags, in single packs, two-packs, or three-packs. K-cups and Nespresso pods are also available in all three roasts.

“¡DÍOS MIO! COFFEE comes from my love of Colombian coffee and my high standards when it comes to the quality of my coffee,” Vergara said in a press release. “I grew up in Colombia, drinking Colombian coffee. We have spent the last few years creating these blends with an amazing team of female coffee growers behind us.”

Vergara also noted that 30 percent of Colombian coffee growers are women. She called it “an honor to highlight their families and legacies.”
