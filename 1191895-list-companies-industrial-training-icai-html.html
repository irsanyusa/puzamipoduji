Сompanies for ICAI industrial training: the official list

The list of companies where ICAI students can pass their training is approved by the government. It means that there are no occasional companies and units and that the list is not subject to change without any serious reason. The training by itself is a win-win strategy for both the students and companies. The students receive the professional practice and skills they need, while companies regularly receive young, energetic and well-trained specialists.

ICAI industrial training eligibility

Knowing the list of available and approved ICAI companies is not everything. You need to be eligible to pass this training. For this, it’s necessary to pass the respective examination first. Then, to be allowed to go through industrial training, a student must be in his or her last year of the previous practical training. These conditions are a must before any industrial training can begin for a person.

If you still have questions or need to know more ICA industrial training details, please, refer to the official website of ICAI and get in touch with them with the help of the contact details they provide.
