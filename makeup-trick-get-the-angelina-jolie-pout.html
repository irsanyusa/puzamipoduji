Fillers are risky. It takes an extremely skilled injector to make your lips appear fuller conservatively, while retaining the natural shape of your lips. But make-up has become a substitute for highlighting and creating illusions of features we are lacking. With make up, a crooked nose could be straightened, cheekbones are created and lips can be made to appear fuller!
