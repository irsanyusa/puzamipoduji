Jaikay steps out with infectious single 'Fine Lady'

June 19, 2024
blog

Collaborating with producer Cozykiyo, Jaikay's 'Fine Lady' is a unique sound that blends Afrobeats and Soul for an exciting Afrosoul sound that soulful melodies and heartfelt lyrics that resonate deeply with listeners.

The single follows the path of quintessential Afrobeats records made to celebrate the beauty of women, which continues to be a source of major inspiration to generations of artists.

Jaikay's entrance into the music industry marks a landmark moment for the young artist who always wanted to make music at an early age, despite the challenges of her traditional Muslim upbringing.

Influenced by Inspired by Nigerian legends like 2face, Tiwa Savage, Wizkid, Fela Kuti, and Burna Boy, Jaikay's passion for music was evident as she transitioned from a potential football career to the studio.

With her debut single 'Fine Lady', Jaikay wishes to captivate audiences with her music and cultivate a fanbase that will join her on the journey to making a name for herself as one of the most talented new artists in Afrobeats.
