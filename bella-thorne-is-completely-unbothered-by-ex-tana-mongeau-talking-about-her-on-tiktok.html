In a recent viral TikTok video, Bella Thorne kept a nonchalant demeanor while reacting to her ex, Tana Mongeau, talking about her. The video, which has garnered over 8.7 million views at the time of writing, captures Thorne’s laid-back response to Mongeau’s narration about Thorne’s pet wolf.

Bella Thorne Posted a Viral TikTok Reacting to Tana Mongeau

In the TikTok video, Mongeau shares an amusing yet peculiar incident from her past relationship with Thorne. Living in the hills, Thorne allegedly took an unconventional approach to address the threat of coyotes to her cats, by acquiring a pet wolf.

Throughout Mongeau’s storytelling, Thorne remains completely unbothered. She remains mostly silent, casually applying mascara to her eyelashes. Despite the potentially shocking nature of the tale, Thorne responds with a simple revelation of her own, she got a “catio,” a cat patio.

What sets this TikTok exchange apart is Thorne’s seemingly unbothered reaction to Mongeau’s narrative. While Mongeau unfolds a story involving a pet wolf, Thorne interjects with a lighthearted comment about her own feline-friendly addition to her home.

@bellathorne #duet with @Tana Mongeau ♬ original sound – Tana Mongeau

The TikTok video shines a light on the dramatic and unconventional aspects of Thorne and Mongeau’s past relationship. Their ongoing public interactions, even after the breakup, continue to intrigue social media audiences.

Bella Thorne Once Released a Diss Track Directed to Tana Mongeau

Amid the ongoing public drama between Thorne and her ex, Mongeau, an old wound resurfaces with the release of Thorne’s 2020 single, “SFB (Stupid F*cking B*tch).” Although the song was unveiled during their breakup, its recent resurgence on social media has reignited the feud.

The track’s lyrics, which avoid explicitly naming Mongeau, feature cutting lines such as you’re a stupid f*cking b*tch, but you already know this” and “You wanted me for clout, and I should’ve thrown you out.” Thorne’s online promotion of the song has added fuel to the fire, with cryptic tweets about someone attempting to stay relevant and questions about their ability to attract attention.

Mongeau, known for her quick and witty responses, promptly engages in the digital sparring match. In a series of tweets, she questions Thorne’s motives and relevance, countering with statistics about the popularity of her own recent tweets.

Adding a modern twist to their feud, Mongeau shares a TikTok featuring Thorne’s ‘SFB,’ expressing disbelief at the ongoing situation. The conflict takes a more serious turn as Mongeau retweets fan sentiments, implying that Thorne is still fixated on her, supported by a video quote from Nicki Minaj.
