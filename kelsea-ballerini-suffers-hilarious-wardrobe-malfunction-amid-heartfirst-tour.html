Country singer Kelsea Ballerini recently experienced a funny wardrobe malfunction while on her Heartfirst tour. Amid the awkward moment though, she’s found a way to redeem herself from it.

Kelsea Ballerini Bears Wardrobe Malfunction During Tour

In the middle of her performance in Milwaukee, Wisconsin, as part of her Heartfirst tour, Ballerini was shocked when fans suddenly pointed out something with her outfit. It turned out that a black and gray sock was stuck in her in her fringe jacket.

Ballerini was singing when fans shouted words she apparently didn’t hear. To clarify, she responded, “What’s wrong?” To which the fans squealed altogether, “There’s a sock!”

The “Blindsided” singer was then wearing a gold and glittery romper, complimented by a color-matching cowboy boots. As she was also wearing a gold-beaded fringe jacket, the sock was visibly dangling off. Soon after she noticed this wardrobe blunder, she grabbed the sock, held it up, and blabbered a funny line.

“I feel like Sully from Monsters Inc., There’s a sock on my jacket” she said. “Guys, I’m the least cool person you’ve ever seen, but now, now I relate to Monsters Inc.“

She then threw the sock out of the stage and proceeded to perform her next song. On Instagram, Ballerini reflected on the hilarious malfunction, saying that people really “never know what may happen” during concerts like Heartfirst tour.

She Announces Relationship With Chase Stokes

Around the course of Ballerini’s tour, it has also been confirmed that she and Chase Stokes are officially dating. This confirmation cam two months after the couple kept coy amid certain speculations.

Appearing in Today for an interview, Stokes nodded with a “mhm” when host Sheinelle Jones referenced his relationship with the country star. Moreover, when Jones called them as “beautiful couple,” the Outer Banks actor replied, “Thank you.”

In the same interview, Stokes also admitted that he’s a fan of Ballerini’s music.

“Absolutely I’m a fan,” he said. “Yeah, who isn’t a fan?”

Ballerini filed divorce from her ex-husband Morgan Evans back in August 2022. The two lasted for almost five years. Be that as it may, her fans are happy now that Ballerini has found a new beau.
