Chris Hemsworth Sends His Wife Elsa Pataky a Sweet Message on Her Birthday

August 14, 2024
blog

Considering the current wave of Hollywood breakups and divorces, it warms to see other celeb couples whose relationships are getting stronger. One example of such a power couples is Chris Hemsworth and Elsa Pataky, who recently celebrated Elsa’s birthday together. Chris couldn’t leave his wife without a sweet message on this occasion.

Chris Hemsworth congratulated his wife on her 47th birthday.

Chris Hemsworth took to Instagram to celebrate his wife Elsa Pataky’s 47th birthday with a heartfelt tribute. He shared photos from their birthday celebration, expressing his love and appreciation for his “partner in crime.”

In the Instagram post, Chris sweetly captioned the carousel of pictures with, “Happy birthday to my partner in crime, love you always, here’s to many more.”

One of the photos in the carousel was a video capturing a beautiful moment at a restaurant. Elsa Pataky was seated before a candlelit chocolate and strawberry-covered birthday cake. At the same time, Chris Hemsworth and some friends sang the classic birthday song, “Happy Birthday,” to make the occasion even more special.

Pataky and Hemsworth are true examples of the perfect family.

In a recent interview, the 39-year-old actor from Extraction 2, Chris Hemsworth, spoke highly of his wife, Elsa Pataky, with whom he shares three children: their 11-year-old daughter India, and their twin sons Tristan and Sasha, aged 9.

Hemsworth emphasized that their relationship lies in their shared goal of having a good time, finding joy in laughter, and embarking on new adventures together. He praised Elsa for her incredible sacrifices, commitment, hard work, unwavering support, and forgiveness over the years, acknowledging that he wouldn’t have been able to achieve everything he has without her by his side.

“Goes so fast. Like everything. We were having kids at the same time my career was taking off, we were getting married and getting to know each other. It feels like we got to know each other halfway through our relationship five, six years ago. In a beautiful way,” he said. “Once our kids were out of nappies, things became a bit more manageable.”

The couple celebrates their 13th anniversary this year.

It is evident that Chris Hemsworth and Elsa Pataky maintain a strong and enduring marriage for over a decade, proving that their love has stood the test of time.

They first crossed paths in 2010, and their connection was so strong that they decided to tie the knot within a few months. The fast engagement and wedding surprised many fans, but their relationship has flourished ever since.

As they approached their 10th anniversary in 2020, Elsa Pataky spoke candidly to Australian magazine about their marriage. She acknowledged that, like any other couple, they have experienced ups and downs. Despite the perception of being a picture-perfect couple in the public eye, she emphasized that their relationship requires continuous effort and commitment.

“It’s funny that people think of us as a perfect couple,” she shared. “No way. It’s been ups and downs, and we keep working on the relationship.”

Despite his popularity, Chris Hemsworth is very down-to-earth man. He is renowned for his heartwarming interactions with fans, frequently engaging with them during shooting breaks. One memorable moment was when a photograph of him with his fans in India went viral, capturing his genuine happiness.
