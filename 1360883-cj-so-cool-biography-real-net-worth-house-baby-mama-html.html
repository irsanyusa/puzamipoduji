The American YouTube star was born on March 29, 1989, in Gary, Indiana. His childhood was not a bed of roses. His mother was a drug addict, and his father died while in prison.

Cool went to Theodore Roosevelt High School in his hometown. He later transferred to William A Wirt High School, from where he matriculated. He joined the Navy and was assigned to the Presidential Guard when he was 19 years old.

Cool later on enrolled at Purdue University to study Criminology. He dropped out in 2012 and started looking for jobs to support himself.

Career

CJ So Cool sought jobs in several restaurants such as Chuck E Cheese and Denny’s. His shifts were often long, but that did not dampen his mood as he used to impress his co-workers with his uncanny sense of humor.

Through the encouragement of his peers, CJ opted to try pursuing his passion for making people laugh. CJ created his YouTube channel on December 26, 2014, but did not start posting videos right away.

CJ’s brother, Jinx, was into YouTube reaction videos and influenced his older brother to focus on similar content. As a result, he jumped from thousands of views to hundreds of millions of views within a short time.
