Music superstar Jason Derulo is set to bring his incredible talent and industry experience to The Voice AU as a coach in the upcoming 2023 season. The official Instagram page of the popular singing competition revealed the exciting news in a post that featured the four coaches for the season.

Jason Derulo Joins the Cast of The Voice AU

Derulo’s addition to the cast is a significant move that is expected to inject fresh energy and a new perspective to the long-running competition. The music star is a chart-topping artist with a wealth of experience in the industry. Which makes him an ideal candidate to mentor aspiring artists.

The previously mentioned post featured other coaches that will be appearing alongside Derulo, which includes Jessica Mauboy, Rita Ora, and Guy Sebastian. In a statement, Derulo expressed his enthusiasm for joining the show. He then stated that he is honored to be part of the tradition of discovering and nurturing exceptional talent.

The singer’s impressive resume speaks for itself. With 12 platinum singles, over 25 million album sales, and collaborations with some of the biggest names in the music industry. His expertise and experience in the industry, coupled with his talent as a dancer and choreographer, make him an ideal fit for the show.
