Full Name: Kane Allen Brown
Profession: Country Singer

Biography: Kane Brown is an American singer, who was the first artist to have simultaneous number ones on all five of the main Billboard country charts. He later released his second studio album, Experiment (2018), which reached number one of the Billboard 200.

Brown first came to fame with his homemade videos on Facebook, in which he performed covers of songs by names such as Brantley Gilbert, Billy Currington, and Alan Jackson. He then transitioned into reducing studio albums after a Kickstarted provided the funding for his first EP, titled Closer (2015).
